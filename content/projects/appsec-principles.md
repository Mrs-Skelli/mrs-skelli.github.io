Inspired by OWASP's Secure Coding Dojo, **AppSec Principles** is a CTF aimed at beginners who want to learn secure coding concepts without needing prior CTF experience.

The goal is simple: educate anyone playing about secure coding principles they can carry into their daily work. Built with S4t4n, Night, Akwa, Sociallyencrypted, Craig!x, and many more contributors.
